# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
## Project: Kilangi Jewellery – Homepage (Web)

---

## 1. Product Overview
- Kilangi is a **premium gold and silver jewellery e-commerce brand**
- Focuses on:
  - Timeless design
  - Ethical sourcing
  - Lab-grown gold and silver
- Homepage is the **primary conversion driver**
- Goal is to turn visitors into:
  - Shoppers
  - Gift buyers
  - Brand believers

---

## 2. Problem Statement
- Users want:
  - Elegant jewellery without trust issues
  - Clear pricing and authenticity
  - Easy gifting options
- Current gap:
  - Premium jewellery sites often feel intimidating
  - Ethical and lab-grown value is not always explained clearly
- This homepage must:
  - Build trust fast
  - Showcase value visually
  - Push users toward exploration and purchase

---

## 3. Target Users
- Primary Users:
  - Women aged 22–45
  - Buying for self or gifting
- Secondary Users:
  - Men buying gifts (birthdays, anniversaries, engagements)
- User mindset:
  - Emotion-driven
  - Trust-sensitive
  - Design-aware

---

## 4. Key Goals
- Make users **feel confident and inspired**
- Highlight:
  - Bestseller products
  - Lab-grown jewellery benefits
  - Ethical sourcing
- Reduce friction to:
  - Browse collections
  - Shop by price
  - Buy gifts

---

## 5. Core Features (Homepage)

### Navigation
- Top navigation with:
  - Gold, Silver, Gifting, About, Contact
  - Search bar for quick discovery

### Hero Section
- Emotional headline: *“Because You Deserve to Feel Timeless”*
- Clear CTA: **Explore the Elegance**
- Reinforces brand promise instantly

### Product Discovery
- Bestseller carousel
- Categories:
  - Rings, Earrings, Pendants, Bracelets, Bangles
- Price visibility with discounts

### Trust Builders
- Anti-fade polish
- Lightweight comfort
- BIS Hallmark
- 925 Sterling Silver
- Free 15-day return and exchange

### Gifting Section
- Shop under price ranges:
  - ₹10,000
  - ₹20,000
  - ₹30,000
- E-gift cards
- Personalized gift notes

### Brand Ethos
- Ethical sourcing
- Sustainable processes
- Lab-grown gold and silver explained simply

### Social Proof
- Customer testimonials
- Repeated reviews to reinforce trust

---

## 6. Success Metrics
- Homepage bounce rate ↓
- Product clicks ↑
- Add-to-cart rate ↑
- Gift section engagement ↑

---

## 7. Constraints & Assumptions
- Desktop-first design (mobile can follow later)
- Visual storytelling is critical
- Performance must stay fast despite rich visuals

---

## 8. Future Enhancements (Forward-Looking)
- AI-powered product recommendations
- Occasion-based gift quiz
- Virtual try-on for jewellery
- Loyalty and referral system

---

## 9. Out of Scope (For Now)
- Mobile app
- International shipping
- AR features at launch
